Our Mission

Founded by a group of agronomists and AI researchers, AgriVision AI was born out of a simple observation: billions of dollars in crops are lost annually to preventable diseases.

We believe that every farmer, regardless of the size of their land, should have access to world-class diagnostic tools. Our technology empowers growers to make data-driven decisions that save money and reduce chemical waste.

What We Stand For

Sustainability

By detecting issues early, we help farmers use 30% less pesticide on average.

Scientific Rigor

Our models are verified by plant pathologists to ensure laboratory-level accuracy.

Accessibility

Designed to work on low-end smartphones in remote areas with poor internet.
